The main metric used in these visualisations is called the HPI, Historical Popularity Index, it is calculated by aggregrating information on an individuals online Popularity. It is currently made up of five components: age, number Wikipedia language editions, the concentration of pageviews across languages, the stability of pageviews over time, and the number of non-English pageviews. It was derived by the people at https://pantheon.world/ as well as the data source used in this project.
